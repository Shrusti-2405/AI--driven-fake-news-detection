About Factify

This project, Factify, is a powerful demonstration of modern AI capabilities applied to the critical task of fake news detection and fact-checking. It was developed by K. Anantha Krishna Rao, Shrusti P, Anushriya R, Richa Kumari as part of an Internship from IEEE CS society.

The system leverages a locally-run Large Language Model to analyze claims.

This entire application, from the Python backend with Flask to the interactive front-end, showcases a complete end-to-end AI product development cycle.
